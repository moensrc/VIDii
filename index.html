Hatch a Yoshi!

1990

  • Console: SNES

    Yoshi made his first appearance in Super Mario World. Based on the idea of Mario being able to ride a horse, Yoshi is a character designed to be controlled by Mario.

    Characteristics

    • Long neck and tiny body
    • Round spikes
    • Orange hands

    Abilities

    • Eat enemies with tongue
    • Absorb Koopas powers
    • Jump on enemies

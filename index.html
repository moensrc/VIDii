Hatch a Yoshi!

1990

  • Console: SNES

    Yoshi made his first appearance in Super Mario World. Based on the idea of Mario being able to ride a horse, Yoshi is a character designed to be controlled by Mario.

    Characteristics

    • Long neck and tiny body
    • Round spikes
    • Orange hands

    Abilities

    • Eat enemies with tongue
    • Absorb Koopashells powers
    • Jump on enemies

  • Console: SNES

    Yoshi races against 7 other characters from the Super Mario title games. Yoshi is a Lightweight character in this line-up, therefore he has high acceleration.

    Characteristics

    • Long neck
    • Longer arms

    Abilities

    • Throw a bouncing obstacle that makes other players slip
    • Use Powerups
    • Power Slide or Drift

  • Console: SNES

    Play as differently colored Yoshi's and lead Baby Mario around this platformer adventure. This game is the first wherein Yoshi can primarily be controlled instead of Mario.

    Characteristics

    • Shorter neck
    • Bigger nose and head

    Abilities

    • Lay a Yoshi Egg
    • Throw Yoshi Egg
    • Flutter Jump

  • Console: SNES

    Visit Yo'ster Isle and meet Yoshi here. Participate with Yoshi in the Mushroom derby race after beating Boshi in a one-on-one race.

    Characteristics

    • Red shoes
    • Defined cheeks
    • Bigger saddle

    Abilities

    • Eat Yoshi Cookies for speed!
    • Give Mario Yoshi Candy

  • Console: N64

    When collecting the full 120 stars in Super Mario 64, Mario can meet Yoshi on the rooftop. Yoshi thanks the player for playing the game. The fun isn't over yet!

    Characteristics

    • Poly shapes
    • Thin neck
    • Big nose

    Abilities

    • Jumping off the roof for no reason
    • Give Mario max. 100 1UPS

  • Console: N64

    With a more humanoid Yoshi, travel through levels in the form of pages of a pop-up storybook. Eat Fruits to complete the platform levels.

    Characteristics

    • Rounder shapes with pixels
    • Short, stubby neck
    • Standing up straight

    Abilities

    • Eat Fruit
    • Sniffing to find underground items
    • Throw Eggs

  • Console: GameCube

    Help Mario complete levels by collecting Shine Sprites. Yoshi changes colors based on the fruits he last ate. Spray juice on enemies for special abilities!

    Characteristics

    • Long arms with big hands
    • Orange shoes
    • Partially closed eyes

    Abilities

    • Eat Fruit & spray fruit juice
    • Flutter Jump
    • Eat Enemies

  • Console: Nintendo DS

    Instead of Mario, control Yoshi through the levels of a remastered Super Mario 64. This is the first and only time Yoshi is fully controllable in a 3D-environment game.

    Characteristics

    • Humanoid
    • Round shapes
    • Closed hands

    Abilities

    • Spit Fire with Power Flower
    • Throw Eggs
    • Change into different characters

  • Console: WiiU

    Explore the textile and fabric environment of Craft Island. All but two Yoshi's were cursed into Wonder Wool by Bowser Jr... Knit all the yarn Yoshi's back together!

    Characteristics

    • Crocheted of yarn
    • Lack of hands

    Abilities

    • Make yarn balls
    • Collect Wonder Wool
    • Eat enemies

  • Console: Switch

    Explore a hand-crafted world from the front- and flipside with Yoshi's of multiple colors. Reminded of toys from kindergarten, help Yoshi complete them and bring the Sundream Stone back!

    Characteristics

    • Plush texture (flocky)
    • High Quality
    • The Yoshi we know and love

    Abilities

    • Throw Eggs
    • Flutter Jump
    • Eat enemies
